Gardening & Service FAQs

What areas do you service?

We primarily serve the New York City area, including Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and surrounding boroughs. Please contact us for requests outside these areas.

Do you offer eco-friendly gardening options?

Yes, sustainability is a core principle. We emphasize native planting, organic fertilizers, and water conservation practices in all projects.

How long does a typical garden design consultation last?

Consultations typically last between one to two hours depending on the scope and your questions.

Can you help with seasonal garden maintenance?

Absolutely! We offer tailored seasonal maintenance packages to keep your garden thriving year-round.

Is there a minimum service charge?

Pricing depends on project size and services requested. Contact us for a detailed quote.
