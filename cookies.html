What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They enable the website to recognize your device and store information such as preferences or session details.

Types of Cookies

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies stored on your device for a set period or until manually deleted.
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by the website you're visiting directly.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by domains other than the website you're visiting, typically for analytics or advertising.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site usage, and provide personalized content. Our uses include:

  • Remembering your cookie preferences and consent choices.
  • Maintaining your session so you don't need to log in repeatedly.
  • Analyzing user behavior to improve website performance and services.
  • Personalizing content and targeting relevant advertisements.

Types of Cookies Used by PetalVibes Gardening

Managing and Controlling Cookies

You can control cookies via your browser settings to accept, reject, or delete cookies. How to do this depends on your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies and site data

Note that disabling cookies may impact website functionality and degrade your user experience.

Cookie Consent Dialog

On your first visit to our site, you will see a cookie consent dialog at the bottom of the page. You can choose to accept or decline cookies.

- Accepting cookies helps us offer a tailored and seamless browsing experience.
- Declining may restrict some site features but will prevent non-essential cookies from being stored.

How Long Do Cookies Last?

The duration cookies remain on your device depends on the type:

  • Session Cookies: Until the end of your browser session.
  • Persistent Cookies: Can remain for months or years depending on their purpose.

Cookies and Personal Data

Cookies by themselves do not typically contain personally identifiable information unless you have provided it to us directly. However, they help us recognize your device and activity patterns.

Our Privacy Policy details how we collect and protect your personal data and is incorporated by reference into this Cookies Policy.

Cookies in Other Jurisdictions

Different countries have different laws governing cookies and tracking technologies. PetalVibes Gardening complies with applicable regulations, including the EU ePrivacy Directive and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA regarding the collection and use of cookies and personal information. You may request details about the cookies we use and opt out of sale or sharing of your information.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Residents in the European Union enjoy rights under the GDPR, including choosing to reject cookies not strictly necessary for the functioning of the site. You can manage your cookie preferences and withdraw consent at any time.
