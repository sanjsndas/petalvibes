Tips for Maintaining Your Garden

Keeping your garden thriving requires knowledge and routine care. Here are some essential tips from our experts:

  • Water your plants deeply but infrequently to encourage strong root growth.
  • Use mulch to retain soil moisture and suppress weeds, while adding organic matter.
  • Prune trees and shrubs during dormancy to promote healthy growth and flowering.
  • Choose native and drought-resistant plants to reduce water usage and maintenance.
  • Regularly monitor for pests and diseases, adopting eco-friendly control methods.
  • Rotate plantings seasonally to enrich soil and prevent pests.
