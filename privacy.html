Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of utmost importance to PetalVibes Gardening. Please read our comprehensive policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Introduction

Welcome to PetalVibes Gardening. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring that your privacy is respected. Please read this document carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information and how we treat it.

By using our website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. The types of information we collect include:

1. Personal Identification Information

This includes your name, email address, phone number, postal address, and other identifiers you provide when contacting us or subscribing to our services.

2. Usage Data

We collect information automatically through your interactions with our website, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, time and date of visits, and other diagnostic data.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze site usage, and personalize content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Communication Data

Any communications you send to us via email, contact forms, or messaging platforms may be collected and stored for customer service and record-keeping purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Providing, operating, and maintaining our website and services.
  • Responding to inquiries, comments, and support requests.
  • Sending newsletters, updates, and promotional materials related to PetalVibes Gardening.
  • Improving user experience by analyzing usage trends and site functionality.
  • Protecting our website and users by monitoring for fraud and unauthorized activities.
  • Complying with legal obligations and law enforcement requests.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to collect information to improve your experience on our site. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. Types of cookies we use include:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the operation of the website, such as session management and security.

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website to help us improve functionality and user experience.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make, like language preferences.

Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by advertising partners to build profiles and deliver relevant ads.

You can control cookies through your browser settings and opt-out of certain tracking mechanisms.

Cookies Detailed Explanation

Cookies help make your interaction with our website more efficient. There are several types:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device to remember preferences.
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by our website directly.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by services we embed or integrate with (e.g., analytics).

Managing cookies is possible through your browser settings where you can reject, accept, or delete cookies anytime.
